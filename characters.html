Characters

Jayce

Leader of the Lighting League

Jayce is a brave and determined young hero on a quest to find his father, Audric, and stop the evil Monster Minds. He wields the magical root known as the "Root of Light," which is key to defeating the Monster Minds.

Flora

Plant-based lifeform and member of the Lightning League

Created by Audric, Flora is a humanoid plant with the ability to communicate with other plants. She is compassionate, wise, and plays a vital role in guiding the team and offering insights into their journey.

Herc Stormsailor

Pilot and mercenary

Herc is a seasoned pilot and a bit of a rogue who joins the Lightning League primarily for the promise of payment. Despite his initial selfish motivations, he becomes a loyal and valuable member of the team.

Oon

Magical Eternal Squire

Oon is a small, brave, and loyal robot knight who serves Jayce. Armed with a magical lance, Oon is determined to protect Jayce and assist the Lightning League, often providing comic relief as well.

Gillian

Wizard and mentor

Gillian is an elderly wizard with vast knowledge of magic and science. He acts as a mentor and advisor to Jayce, using his wisdom and magical abilities to aid the Lightning League in their quest.

Saw Boss

Leader of the Monster Minds

Saw Boss is the primary antagonist, a ruthless and cunning leader of the Monster Minds. He commands his mutant plant followers with an iron fist, constantly plotting to conquer the universe and destroy the Lightning League.
